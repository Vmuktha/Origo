ORIGO is a **blockchain-powered supply chain transparency platform** designed to ensure **fair pricing, traceability, and trust** in agricultural produce markets.  
The solution was proposed for **Smart India Hackathon 2025** under the theme **Agriculture, FoodTech & Rural Development** 

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional agricultural supply chains suffer from:
- Price manipulation and farmer exploitation
- No transparency in produce quality or origin
- Delayed payments
- Wastage of low grade produce

ORIGO addresses these issues by combining **AI-based quality grading** with **blockchain backed traceability**.

---

## What ORIGO Does

- Digitally tracks agricultural produce from **farm to buyer**
- Uses **AI models** to grade produce quality
- Records all supply chain events on **blockchain**
- Ensures **tamper proof data** and **fair payments**
- Supports **offline and low network farmers**

---

## How ORIGO Works (Simple Flow)

1. **Farmer** lists produce using app or IVR
2. **AI grading** assigns quality grade with confidence score
3. Each produce lot gets a **unique token + QR code**
4. **Mitra** verifies quality and logistics
5. **Buyers** bid or buy with escrow based payments
6. All events are recorded on the **blockchain**
7. Consumers can scan QR to view complete journey

---

## Key Features

- AI-driven quality grading (vision + RGB + audio)
- Blockchain backed traceability
- Smart contract escrow payments
- QR-based verification
- Offline access via IVR and TFLite
- Zero waste routing for low grade produce
- Audit and monitoring layer for reliability

- **Blockchain:** Polygon / Ethereum smart contracts
